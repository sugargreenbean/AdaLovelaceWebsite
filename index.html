What is the Ada Lovelace Conference?

The Ada Lovelace Conference was born out of the desire to attend a conference full of hands on workshops,
a conference emphasizing hard skills in tech rather than soft skills. We believe that the "leaky pipeline"
needs knowledgable women sharing knowledge about code and technology.

Our goal is to provide an event for mid to senior level engineers to hone their skills.

We welcome anyone who identifies as female and allies to come create at the Ada Lovelace Conference.

One day. Five Tracks. Hands on.

Walk away with new skills from workshops. Choose one track or jump around.
No intro classes, jump right into making the magic happen.

  • Track Topics

    Choose from topics in Software Engineering, Machine Learning, Web Development, Mobile, and Hardware.

  • No career panels

    Learning from people's experiences is cool. Learning by doing is even better.

  • Community

    Meet other people who <3 technology too.
    We are stronger together.
    We are stronger smarter.

  • Code of Conduct

    As event organizers, we will strive to deliver an inclusive event to all based on the CSSConf AU Code of Conduct.
